FAQ

How long will it take for me to receive a response regarding my renewal status?

You should receive a response from your broker within 2 business days.

How long will it take to receive my insurance papers and stickers?

You will receive your insurance papers right away via e-mail. The stickers will be mailed and may take 7-10 business days.

What are the insurance requirements in BC?

All drivers must purchase Basic Autoplan insurance from ICBC. Drivers can select any optional coverages they think would benefit them. For more information, contact your broker.

Is there any cost for at-home renewal?

No. Totally free!

How does your service work?

After submitting a request to renew your insurance, we forward your application to the next available insurance broker. The insurance broker will process your renewal and send you your papers electronically. The entire process can be completed at the comfort of your own home.

What if I made a mistake in my submission?

Please resubmit a new renewal application form. We will use the most recent submission.

What information do you require?

We will need basic personal information (such as your name and address), make and model of vehicle, and license number.
