Coverage Options



Basic Coverage

(mandatory coverage)

  • Medical cost and wage loss for you, your passengers, and members of your household
  • Third party liability coverage
  • Underinsured Motorist Protection
  • Hit-and-run damage and injuries
  • Inverse liability protection

Optional Coverage

  • Collision
    • Cover repair and replacement costs, even if you’re at fault.
    • Even the best drivers can make mistakes and fixing your vehicle can be expensive. Collision coverage helps pay for your vehicle repairs, towing, storage and more, even if the crash was your fault.
  • Comprehensive
    • Covers loss or damage to your vehicle from theft, vandalism, fire, earthquakes, explosions, flying or falling objects, hitting an animal, and weather-related damage.
  • Extended Third-Party Liability
    • Protects you from financial risk in an at-fault crash.
  • Extended Underinsured Motorist Protection
    • Provides coverage for yourself when others don’t have enough coverage.
  • Loss of Use
    • Covers your expenses to rent a vehicle, use taxis and take public transit while your vehicle is being repaired.
